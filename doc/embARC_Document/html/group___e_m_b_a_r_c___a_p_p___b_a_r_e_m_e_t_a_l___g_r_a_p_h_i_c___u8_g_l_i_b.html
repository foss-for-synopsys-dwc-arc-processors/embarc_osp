Usage Manual

  • How to connect SSD1306 to EMSK.
    SSD1306-I2C-SCL <----> J2-PIN3
    SSD1306-I2C-SDA <----> J2-PIN4
    SSD1306-GND <----> J2-PIN5
    SSD1306-DV33 <----> J2-PIN6

  • How to use this example

    This example demonstrates how to use U8glib functions of drawing box, line and ASCII characters. Dynamic graphics are shown in SSD1306. And display width & height information is shown in Tera Term.
